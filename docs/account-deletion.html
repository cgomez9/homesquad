Account Deletion — HomeSquad

App: HomeSquad  ·  Developer: Carlos Gomez  ·  Last updated: 2026-05-27

This page explains how to request deletion of your HomeSquad account and the data associated with it.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to sabiondo3101@gmail.com from the email address tied to your HomeSquad account.
  2. Use the subject line: HomeSquad account deletion.
  3. In the body, you do not need to provide a reason. A single line such as "Please delete my account" is sufficient.
  4. We confirm receipt within 7 days and complete the deletion within 30 days.
We verify deletion requests by sending you a one-time confirmation reply to the email address on the account. This prevents anyone other than you from deleting your account.

What we delete

  • Your authentication record (email, hashed password, or Google identifier).
  • All kid profiles you created (names, avatars, hashed PINs).
  • All chores, rewards, redemptions, achievements, and approval history associated with your family.
  • Your push notification token.
  • Any co-parent invite records you sent or received.

If you co-parent a family and the other parent has not requested deletion, your authentication record is removed and your name/avatar are anonymized on shared records, but the family data the co-parent created is preserved for them.

What we retain

  • Aggregated, non-identifiable logs. Server access logs are retained for up to 90 days for security and abuse-prevention purposes. These logs do not contain account-identifying information after deletion is complete.
  • Email correspondence with you about the deletion request itself. Retained for 12 months as a record that the deletion was completed.

Deletion of individual data without closing the account

You can remove individual kid profiles, chores, and rewards directly from inside the HomeSquad app at any time, without contacting us.
